About her

1

Miley Ray Cyrus, born Destiny Hope Cyrus on november 23 1992, is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. Regarded as one of the few examples of a child star with a successful career as an adult, she has made the Time 100 list in 2008 and 2014 and was ranked as the ninth greatest Billboard 200 female artist ever.

2

She began acting at a young age, landing the starring role on the hit show Hannah Montana in 2004. Cyrus went on to release several successful albums, including Breakout (2008) and Can't Be Tamed (2010) while becoming increasingly known for her wild behavior. Her 2013 single "Wrecking Ball," backed by a controversial video, became her first U.S. No. 1 hit.
